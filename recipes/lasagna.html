Lasagna

Back to recipes

This recipe is itended for an open-minded approach, freestyle the recipe to fit your style. It remains vague intentionally.

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Tomatoes
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Italian sausage
  • Spices (e.g. parsley, oregano, thyme, and pepper are recommended)
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt to taste
  1. Prepare the lasagna noodles in salted water
  2. Brown the Italian sausage
  3. Create the tomato sauce by blending the tomatoes and combining part of the spices and parmesan cheese, salt as needed
  4. Combine the Italian sausage and tomato sauce
  5. Layer the noodles and cover with alternating layers of tomato sauce and ricotta
  6. Top the lasagna with parsley and cover with mozzarella cheese
  7. Cover with aluminum foil
  8. Bake at 375 for 30 mins
  9. Remove foil and continue to bake for 15 more mins, broil as seen fit
